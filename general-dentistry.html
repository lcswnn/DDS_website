General Dentistry

General Dentist in Berwyn, IL

Do you know how often you’re supposed to see a dentist? Just as you need to visit your routine physician once a year, you also need to visit a general dentist twice a year to maintain your oral health. Here at our dental practice, Dr. Garrison provides comprehensive, gentle preventive dental care to families and individuals. She’s been a trusted Berwyn, IL, area dentist for more than two decades. We are happy to provide you and your family with the general dentistry you’re looking for.

What is general dentistry?

A general dentist is someone who provides dental care to patients of all ages. With a general dentist, you never outgrow us, which means that you have a dentist for life. This can be incredibly comforting for our patients to know that every time they turn to us, they know the type of care they will receive.

Getting regular general dental care every six months is critical for every one of us if we want to maintain healthy smiles. Dr. Garrison and her staff make it easy to keep your smile on track, and we pride ourselves on creating long-lasting and trustworthy relationships with each one of our patients here in Berwyn, IL.

When should I turn to a general dentist?

One of the top benefits of a general dentist is that we offer a variety of dental services all under one roof. Just as it’s convenient to find everything you’re shopping for in one location, it’s just as convenient when you know that your general dentist offers the care and treatments you need when you walk through the doors. Everyone’s needs are different, and we understand that crafting and creating your dream smile is as much an art form as it is about providing the highest quality dentistry. With Dr. Garrison, you will receive a highly skilled dentist and artist who takes great pride in creating gorgeous smiles through cosmetic dentistry.

Some services our general dentist team offer include:

  • Regular dental cleanings, checkups, and x-rays
  • Cosmetic treatments such as teeth whitening and veneers
  • Restorative dentistry such as crowns, root canals, fillings, and dentures
  • Orthodontic treatment including Invisalign
  • Gum disease therapy
  • TMJ disorder treatment

Instead of trying to run around finding a dentist that can address your needs and concerns, you can feel reassured that our general dentistry team can provide your smile with everything it needs to stay healthy.

When was the last time you visited the dentist? If it’s been more than six months, then it’s time to get your smile’s health back on track with a routine checkup. To schedule an appointment with Dr. Garrison, call her Berwyn, IL, practice today at (708) 788-9414!
