Practice Information

A Beautiful Practice For All Your Dental Needs

Facility

We are proud to provide the highest quality dental care available. It is one of our top priorities to protect the well-being of our valued patients. For this reason, our office meets and surpasses all OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) and CDC (Center for Disease Control) standards. We are confident that you will feel right at home in our office as we welcome all patients as if they were family. Our office uses a digital x-ray system which means less exposure to our patients as well as a quick diagnosis. Digital x-rays are also environmentally friendly.

Appointments

If you would like to make an appointment, please contact our office by phone or email, we welcome all patients. Emergency appointments are available, however we do recommend you contact our office to schedule a specific time. We have staff members happy to assist you in Spanish as well.

Financial

We will be more than happy to submit all insurance forms for you and help you recover the most from your benefits. We will do everything we can to help you afford the treatment you need and want. Our office provides short-term payment plans, as well as CareCredit, a convenient financing option. Forms of payment accepted by the office are check, cash, MasterCard, Visa, Discover, CareCredit and American Express.

Insurance

Our office accepts most Insurance plans. We will be glad to assist you in understanding your benefits as well as electronically file your dental claim. Dr. Diane Garrison is a participating provider with Connection Dental, Cigna Dental PPO, and a Premier provider with Delta Dental. Please contact us with any questions you may have in regards to your insurance.

Cancellation

If you are unable to keep an appointment, we ask that you kindly provide us with 24 hours notice. We ask for this advance notice so that we can offer this appointment to another patient. A fee may be charged if a patient does not show up for an appointment without sufficient notice.

We would like to take this opportunity to Thank you for choosing our practice for your dental care!
