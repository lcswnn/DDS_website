Meet Our Team

Dr. Diane Garrison

General Dentist

A healthy beautiful smile is an important part of overall well-being and confidence. Dr Garrison helps her patients achieve it with the utmost care and dedication. Her true passion lies in nurturing the smiles of individuals at every stage of life – whether it's a child's first dental visit, improving aesthetics through whitening and veneers, or restoring chewing function by replacing missing teeth. Each milestone in the journey is significant.

Dr Garrison opened her practice in 1999, and has always enjoyed working with her patients, their families, and a stellar dental team. Her academic journey includes degrees from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and Indiana University School of Dentistry. Always striving to stay well-informed in cutting-edge advancements through Continuing Education, she is proud to be a member of esteemed organizations such as the American Dental Association, Illinois Dental Society, and the Chicago Dental Society.

Rest assured, she is committed to ensuring her patients' overall experience is marked by professionalism, compassion, and kindness every step of the way.

Outside the office, she enjoys Crossfit, her pet rabbit, watching baseball games, and spending quality time with her loved ones.

Char Garrison

Administrative Manager

With 35 years of dental experience, Char's primary goal is to create excellent patient experiences within our dental practice. She excels in fostering patient relationships while coordinating scheduling and logistical details to realize each patient's vision of a perfect smile. Throughout her career, Char has obtained certifications in CPR, prophy/sealants, and many administrative certifications.

Outside of work, she loves going for walks, playing Scrabble, recreating recipes, and spending time with family and close friends.

Elaine Ramirez

Dental Assistant

Elaine joined Dr. Garrison's team in 2017. Proficient in Spanish, she is dedicated to providing comprehensive support to our patients, ensuring their comfort and well-being at every stage of the dental journey. Throughout her career, she has pursued continuous professional development with certifications in polishing, sealant placement, and CPR.

Beyond the realm of dentistry, she is an avid reader and enjoys quality time spent with her family and five cherished feline companions.

Kim Kolpak

Dental Hygienist

Kim has been practicing dental hygiene since 2001, obtaining her degree from Prairie State University. As the newest member of Dr. Garrison's team, joining in 2022, her passion is for helping patients achieve excellent oral health, and delivering exceptional care with a delicate approach. Equipped with certifications in administering local anesthetic, placing sealants, and CPR, she is committed to upholding the highest standards of professional excellence.

Outside of her professional endeavors, she enjoys being in the great outdoors, honing her culinary skills in the kitchen, and cherishing precious moments with her family.

Vicki McDonald

Dental Hygienist

With a dental hygiene career spanning back to 1998, Vicki's passion for patient education is deeply ingrained. She is dedicated to teaching patients the importance of maintaining oral health, particularly in preventing and treating periodontal/gum disease. Certified in CPR, administering local anesthetic, and placing sealants, she prioritize both the safety and comfort of those under her care.

Vicki loves to travel around the globe, immersing herself in diverse cultures, and being a new grandma.
